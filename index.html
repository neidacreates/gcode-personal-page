  • Plant List

Plants are really pretty to have in your home, but they are also good to keep you healthy. Some plants even clean the air you breathe! Having a little bit of nature in your house helps to make your space more alive and that helps you feel happier. That's why it's great to have plants, but it can be hard to take care of them so that they don't die. Make sure to find out what your plants like (Lots of sun or no sun? Lots of water or no water?) and treat them well so they will love you back.
