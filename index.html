Welcome! If you want to learn more about how to raise your own houseplant friends, you've come to the right place!

Plants are not only a beautiful addition to your home, but also an added benefit for your health.

Having a little bit of nature in your house helps to make your space more alive and that helps you feel happier. Some plants even clean the air you breathe!

That's why it's great to have plants, but it can be hard to take care of them so that they don't die. Make sure to find out what your plants like (Lots of sun or no sun? Lots of water or no water?) and treat them well so they will love you back.
